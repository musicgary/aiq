About Us

Who We Are

AIQUANTA Limited is a cutting-edge technology company based in Hong Kong Science and Technology Park. We specialize in the research and development of advanced optoelectronic and semiconductor devices for beam steering and optical switching using non-mechanical components. Our product portfolio includes Silicon Photonics, Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), and other innovative devices catering to the automotive, robotics, 3D packaging, and emerging quantum computing markets.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver innovative optoelectronic solutions that push the boundaries of technology. We aim to enable practical applications of next-generation chips by partnering with leading optoelectronic foundries worldwide, ensuring our customers stay at the forefront of industry advancements.

What We Do

We develop tailor-made electro-optic devices and sub-systems that meet the stringent demands of various sectors including automotive, robotics, computer vision, and quantum computing. Leveraging our expertise in AI, 3D sensing, and LiDAR technologies, we provide professional, highly realistic 3D sensing and virtual reality solutions that serve diverse industries and applications.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert team with extensive experience in AI-driven photonics and semiconductor technologies
  • Strong industry and academic collaboration to ensure cutting-edge innovation
  • Proven track record delivering 3D sensing solutions, LiDAR scanning, and integrated AI systems
